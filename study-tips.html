Many students study hard - but not consistently. These simple tips can help you improve focus and results.

  1. Study at the same time daily. Routine builds habits.
  2. Use a timer. Short sessions (25-30 minutes) work best.
  3. Avoid multitasking. Focus on one subject only.
  4. Take short breaks. Refresh your brain.
  5. Revise weekly. Repetition improves memory.
  6. Track your progress. Seeing results motivates you.
  7. Sleep well. Your brain stores learning during sleep.
